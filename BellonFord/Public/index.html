History

The mention of ghosts or apparations goes well back into history with evidence of their existence being noted by the Egyptians and even the ancient Sumerians. Though little is known about these apparations they have gotten much attention throughout the annals of time. With his writing of Ghosts in the Ancient World Joshua J. Mark (2014) gives a detailed account of how our ancestors viewed these otherworldly deities. From being improperly buried to murder being the cause of their demise; these beings have captured out attention since time immemorial. To say they do not exist would be contrary to the evidence presented otherwise.
